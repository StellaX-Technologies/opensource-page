About Us

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is GAAC Hub?

    GAAC Hub is an open source task management application developed by the GITAM Aero Astro Club. It is designed to enhance collaboration, productivity, and innovation for club members and the broader community of aerospace enthusiasts and project managers.

  • Who can contribute to GAAC Hub?

    Anyone can contribute to GAAC Hub! We welcome developers, designers, and enthusiasts from all backgrounds. Whether you're a seasoned coder or just starting out, your contributions can make a significant impact.

  • How can I get started with GAAC Hub?

    Getting started with GAAC Hub is easy. Simply clone our repository from GitHub, install the necessary dependencies, and run the application. Detailed instructions can be found in the "Getting Started" section of our landing page.

  • What makes GAAC Hub different from other task management tools?

    GAAC Hub offers unique features such as AI-powered task recommendations, an integrated knowledge base, enhanced collaboration tools, and more. These features are designed to provide a superior user experience and meet the specific needs of aerospace and astronomy enthusiasts.

  • Is there a community or support forum for GAAC Hub?

    Yes, we have an active community on GitHub Discussions and Discord server where you can ask questions, share ideas, and get support. You can also follow us on Twitter for updates and announcements.
